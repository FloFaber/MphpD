Filter

Filters provide a way to search for specific songs. They take care of parsing and escaping. They are used in various other methods like [DB::search](../classes/DB#search), [Playlist::add_search](../classes/Playlist#add_search) and more. Refer to the [MPD documentation](https://mpd.readthedocs.io/en/latest/protocol.html#filters) for more information about filters.
